# About This Site

This website serves as our central hub for sharing life updates and memories,
regardless of which social platforms we're active on at any given time.

Rather than scattering our content across multiple services that come and go,
we maintain this permanent home where friends and family can always find us.
Whether we're posting on social media or not,
our most important updates & content live here first.

Our galleries capture the moments that matter to us -
from major life events like our wedding to simple everyday adventures.
We believe in owning our own content and
having a lasting place to preserve these memories.

This approach means you'll always know where to find us online,
no matter how the social media landscape changes.
Bookmark this page and check back whenever you want to see what we're up to!
